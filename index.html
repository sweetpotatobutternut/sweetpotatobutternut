About the Product

Locally Sourced Goodness

Sweet Potato Butternut is a proudly local, health-conscious food made with love and community spirit. Crafted from premium sweet potatoes, it's rich in flavor and nutrition, free from artificial additives, and designed for today’s busy, health-aware consumer.

Versatile & Eco-Friendly

Enjoy it mashed, roasted, or blended into a smoothie. It's packed with fiber, antioxidants, and vitamins. Packaged in biodegradable containers, Sweet Potato Butternut supports your health, local farmers, and the planet—all in one bite.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide nutritious and affordable Sweet Potato Butternut products, while supporting local farmers and promoting wellness in every community.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of healthy sweet potato-based snacks in Partido and beyond, known for sustainability, taste, and impact.
